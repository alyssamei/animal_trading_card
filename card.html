Snow Leopard

Snow leopards have powerful legs and can jump as far as 50 feet.

  • Scientific Name: Panthera uncia
  • Diet: Carnivore
  • Size: 4-5 feet with a tail up to 36 inches
  • Weight: 60 to 120 pounds

Snow leopards have thick white-gray coat spotted with large black rosettes that blend in perfectly with Asia’s steep and rocky, high mountains. This renders them nearly invisible granting their nickname as the “ghost of the mountains.”
